An Hallowen Adventure With Paper and P5

Dark Is The Night There Is An Eye ! Additional Visual Elements A pair of eyes We Are Being Watched. Don't wake it up.

These pages are written as an activity for kids. They are designed to explore visual creation and expression with both paper, scissors, glue/tape and computer. The paper creations are used to explain the simple principles used to make computer graphics through programs.

The programming is in Javascript, using P5.js.
