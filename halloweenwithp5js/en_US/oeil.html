A white ellipse can be cut out from a sheet of white paper. The same can be done with the computer: a shaped filled with white can be added to our black canvas.

An additional black disk can be cut out of black paper (it is easier to make small disks with a hole puncher). As earlier, the same can be achived with the computer: a disk filled with black can be added on the top of the white shape we just added.

Where the eyes are looking can be expressed very simply, by just moving the inner disk.

The first eye can be replicated on the right, creating a pair of eyes glowing in the dark.

Eyes in the dark !

An eye in the dark can be represented as a white ellipse, or disk, on a black background and a smaller black circle in that white ellipse.
